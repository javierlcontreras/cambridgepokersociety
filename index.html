Cambrigde Poker Society

Cambridge Poker Society

We are the official Cambridge Poker Society. We will be running regular events during and outside of term. These events include low stake cash games, tournaments as well as sponsored trips to play tournaments in London for our members. Membership is free and open to all students of the University.

We are currently solely sponsored by Jane Street, a global quantitative trading firm. We encourage all our members to apply to Jane Street. They offer internships in various roles including quantitative trading and software engineering. Poker as a discipline shares many common skills with trading, so please take a look at their website if this is something that would interest you!

To contact us, please email the president using the email tdr36@cam.ac.uk
